I wanna host [[this vault]] for the fun of it, and even if no one’s listening it feels like a good idea to learn with the garage door open. A quick google tells me that the best option is to use [Quartz](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz) to convert my obsidian vault into a static site. Quartz it’s self is very simple it’s just a tool that builds a static sitemap, the best move after that is to just write those site files to an S3 bucket and host them through a cloud front cdn. One thing that I thought was strange is that the links to other pages don’t work out of the box when using just vanilla cloudfront ahead of S3. It was because the links need to contain .html.
